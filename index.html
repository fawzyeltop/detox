Start Your Detox

As the first mental wellness management tool, Detox is a revolutionary mental wellness tool that combines AI-driven technology with alternative practices to track, coach and keep you accountable in prioritizing your mental well-being.

Detox is the first AI-driven mental wellness app designed to actively measure and enhance your mental well-being. Detox addresses the mental struggles and stress-related symptoms stemming from the go-getter, high-pressure startup culture.

High Performers

High Performers

Detox is for the high-performer. You are not looking for advice, tips or validating sentiments. You are looking to transform your inner discipline and leverage group accountability while addressing stress-related symptoms and adopting a more harmonious work-life balance.

Accountability

Create the space to meet and talk with other founders while helping you stay present and accountable in your wellness pursuits with features like pulse checks, proprietary self-assessments, points, badges and leaderboards.

Live Progress

Weekly programming focused on healing and balance (understanding triggers). Earn Detox points via consistency and daily progress.

Framework

A personalized self-care model to keep you engaged.

Retreats

Wide range of holistic healing traditions, restorative therapies and real-life outdoor adventures.

Detox is the catalyst to rising above mental barriers. We understand the negative implications and societal pressures of starting a company and trying to meet modern expectations. We believe transforming our relationship with mental wellness is key to forming the next generation of conscious startup founders.
